About Me

My name is Sunny Long, I am a programming student at Seneca College. This website is the final assessment of the course WEB222. Aeroplane Chess is my favourite board in my childhood. It is easy to learn. The colours made it suitable for children in elementary schools. Also, this game is more than just luck base, it requires some logical thinking and strategies. It is the perfect board game for children.
